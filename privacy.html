Blitzy Privacy Policy Last updated: 2025-09-15

Blitzy is a Chrome extension that helps you manage tabs, track time spent per site, and keep your browsing simple and productive.

What data does Blitzy process?

Blitzy processes a minimal set of local-only data to power its features:

  • Web history (limited): time spent per domain (e.g., youtube.com – 20 minutes).
  • User activity (limited): whether a tab is active or inactive for timing purposes.

This information is stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage.local API and is never transmitted to external servers.

What data does Blitzy not collect?

  • Personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email, address)
  • Financial, payment, or authentication data
  • Health information or personal communications
  • Precise location data
  • Website content (text, images, page contents)

How is the data used?

  • To display “Time Spent” stats in the extension’s popup.
  • To adapt a single, clearly labeled Sponsored banner to general interest categories (e.g., music, shopping, video). This selection also happens locally.

Your choices & control

  • Clear Stats: You can reset all time-tracking data from the popup at any time.
  • Remove Blitzy: Uninstalling the extension deletes all locally stored data.

Data sharing & selling

Blitzy does not sell, rent, or transfer user data to third parties. Blitzy does not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the extension’s single purpose. Blitzy does not use data for creditworthiness or lending.
